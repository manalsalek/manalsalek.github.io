More about me
The Quintessential Mind is an intellectual blog that focuses on self-development, philosophy, psychology, culture, and business. It was created by me, Adrian Iliopoulos, in an attempt to make sense of the world around me. Each article braids the complexity of the big questions with an elaborate and well-articulated narrative. My hope is that through my writings people will discover their truth, embrace reason and logic and lead more interesting and meaningful lives. The results so far have not disappointed. Enjoy the journey.
